# ADR-009: Human Review and Approval Governance

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-03-05
- Owners: Compliance, Corporate Governance
- Related: docs/architecture/public-baseline.md Section 8, AGENTS.md Sections 3.9 and 12

## Context
Sustainability reports require controlled sign-off and SLA-tracked approvals.

## Decision
Approval policy:
- Sustainability Committee draft approval
- Governance committee pre-board review
- Board final approval for publish
- SLA timers, reminders, and escalation rules are mandatory

## Consequences
- Strong governance control
- Longer cycle time in overloaded approval queues

## Verification
- Workflow tests must validate routing order and SLA escalation events.
- Publish must remain blocked without complete signing chain.
